<h2>📌 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
<strong>Bathroom & Kitchen Renovation Cork</strong> is a business website developed for a company specializing in
new installations and renovation of bathrooms and kitchens.
</p>

<p>
The website provides a clean and engaging user experience, helping customers explore services, view renovation ideas,
and easily contact the company for their projects.
</p>

<hr/>

<h2>🚀 Features</h2>

<ul>
  <li>🏠 Modern and professional homepage</li>
  <li>🛁 Bathroom renovation services section</li>
  <li>🍽️ Kitchen renovation services section</li>
  <li>🧱 New construction and remodeling solutions</li>
  <li>📸 Project showcase / gallery</li>
  <li>📞 Contact form with backend integration</li>
  <li>📱 Fully responsive design</li>
  <li>⚡ Fast and optimized performance</li>
  <li>🎯 Clean and user-friendly UI/UX</li>
</ul>

<h2>🛠️ Tech Stack</h2>

<ul>
